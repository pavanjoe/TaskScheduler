About Us!

Planify- the only Task Schedular you need!

Manage your tasks Easily

Have you ever felt like your daily life has becoming overwhelming with goals and things to do that day? Planify is here for you friend! Planify is a website to schedule and manage these tasks in an effiecient and organized manner. Create, delete, and update your tasks as you like. You can even give due dates and categorize them as per how important it is! Mark tasks as completed, and why not set reminders for certain tasks as well.
